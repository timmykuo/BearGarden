Parent Workshops

Bear Garden offers workshops on Personal Emotion Management, Couple Relationships, and Parenting. These are the great opportunities for us, old or young, to grow better together. All the workshops will be conducted by Mr. Stanley Kuo, our school counselor and Mrs. Esther Kuo. If you are interested, please feel free to let Mrs. Kuo know.
