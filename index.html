Dear parents,

After searching so hard for a good preschool for your beloved son/daughter, are you still puzzled which one is the best for him/her? Being a mother of three boys, I have been through that situation several times. I know exactly how you are feeling.


With my enthusiasm and passion in children's education, I am running a friendly home-based preschool, applying creative teaching method as well as professionally designed materials, including the famous Kindermusik, Character First Education and JELIC Intelligence Development Curriculum.

More importantly, they will be delivered to your child with abundant love and care in a clean and safe environment. I emphasize a lot on character building, which for sure will benefit your beloved one life long! You are cordially invited to share the blessings of Bear Garden! Please come to visit us, and you may find that this preschool is exactly the right one for your precious one : ) May God bless your family abundantly...

with much love and hope,
Mrs. Esther Kuo, 2002
