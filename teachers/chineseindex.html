Bear Garden Teachers

Our teachers all share a single passion: loving children! Teaching children demands skills, while loving them involves complete devotion. Every teacher pours out his/her heart to nurture each and every precious little soul brought by God to this lovely garden! It is truly our desire to bring out the very best in them! :)
